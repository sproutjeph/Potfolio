## What it is

ComplyNG is a continuous-compliance operating system for Nigerian startups. Every regulator your startup actually touches — NDPC, CBN, NCC, SEC, NITDA, FIRS, CAC, PENCOM — mapped, monitored, and attestable in one dashboard.

## What it does

- **Deterministic rules engine** maps your business profile to the obligations you're on the hook for
- **Claude Sonnet 4.5** generates the evidence prompts and policy drafts; every artefact is reviewable
- **sha256-hashed evidence** with timestamped chain-of-custody — what was filed, when, by whom
- **One-click NIST OSCAL export** gives regulators a verifiable, machine-readable attestation rather than a PDF

## Stack

Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, Claude Sonnet 4.5, NIST OSCAL, Zod for the rules schema.

## Why it matters

Nigerian founders lose weeks per quarter to overlapping regulator audits. ComplyNG turns "are we compliant?" from a 40-person-hour question into a dashboard glance, and gives regulators something better than a manila folder.
