## What it is

DeeepSolve is an AI-powered exam prep platform for Nigerian secondary school students preparing for JAMB, WAEC, and NECO. It pairs a CBT (computer-based test) simulator with an AI tutor so students can practice under exam conditions and immediately understand *why* an answer is right or wrong.

## What it does

- **CBT simulator** — full timing, randomised questions, real exam paper structure across JAMB/WAEC/NECO
- **10,000+ past questions** indexed and explained
- **Instant AI explanations** — every question has a Gemini/GPT-generated walkthrough that breaks down the working step-by-step
- **Scan-and-solve** — point a phone camera at a textbook question, get a worked solution
- **Offline access** — questions and explanations cache to the device so students with patchy connectivity can still study
- **Audio study notes** — TTS-generated summaries that students listen to during commutes

## Stack

Next.js 15, TypeScript, Postgres, Docker, GCP, Vercel for web. React Native + Expo for the Android app. Multi-LLM routing for the AI tutor.

## Outcomes

50,000+ enrolled students, 85% reported pass rate, top-rated Nigerian exam-prep app on the Play Store.
