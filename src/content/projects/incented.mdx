**Role** — Engineer (contributor)
**Year** — 2025

## What it is

Incented is web3 infrastructure for transparent, fair, incentive-aligned collaboration — a "human coordination protocol" for communities that run grants, bounties, and contributor programs.

## What it does

- Every submission is **tracked on-chain**
- Every vote has **consequences** (skin-in-the-game review)
- Every reward is **transparent** and auditable end-to-end
- Programs can be permissionless or gated, with the same primitive

## Stack

Next.js + TypeScript on the application layer. Supabase for off-chain state. Solidity contracts for the on-chain coordination primitives.

## My role

Contributing engineer — shipped product surfaces, contract integration, and parts of the submission/voting flow.
